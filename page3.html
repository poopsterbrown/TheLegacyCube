FAQ

Throughout the years, Mike has shared the idea of The Legacy Cube with other people, and has occasionally even demenstrated some of the prototypes, as well as making a proof-of-concept demo that wasn't composed of any of The Legacy Cube content, but would give an idea of what a final build would look and feel like.

This collection of FAQs is based on those people. 

Q: Can I download and run The Legacy Cube off of a flash drive? 

A: Yes! Just be sure to format the flash drive in NTFS before hand as The Legacy Cube exceeds 4GB in size.  

Q: I already downloaded a pervious version of The Legacy Cube. Do I need to redownload the whole thing again?

A: The Legacy Cube is no longer able to self-update. If a newer version is available then that means there's new content and/or fixes of known issues (refer to the changelog) and you will wan to use the latest version. Be sure to delete your current version before redownloading to ensure you have enough hard drive space.

Q: Does The Legacy Cube do more harm than good in regards to the grieving process? 

A: Everyone grieves differently. If reliving moments and seeing a lost loved one talk about their life and the people who had an impact on them helps you, then you're in the right place.  

Q: I downloaded The Legacy Cube, but when I try to start it Windows says, "Windows Protected Your Computer" and wont start the application.

A: The Legacy Cube is an independent application that was created by Mike Doss and not a company. Therefore it doesn't have a signed certificate as proof of where it came from. The Legacy Cube is perfectly safe and doesn't contain any malicious software. Click "more info" when you see this message, and then click "Run Anyway" and you will no longer have any problems.

Q: What do I do if some of the videos freeze or don't play at all?


A: There's two solutions that have been found. 
First: press the 'enter' or 'esc' key to exit the video and try again.
Second: Use your mouse to click 1-3 seconds ahead while the video is playing.
There's a lot of content on The Legacy Cube, and it's not all perfect.  

Q: What if I don't have a login? 

A: The Legacy Cube has a "Guest" login. It is very limited as to what you'll be able to view; however, some of the things you'll still be able to see is Mike's final message to you...Unless you're a family member. You'll need a login for that.  

Q: Can I still get a login?

A: If you're seeing this website then than means Mike is already dead, and the available version of The Legacy Cube will be all there ever is. You should have gave him his credentials when he offered this to EVERYONE. Don't worry though, there's still a lot of content for you to see.
