Changelog

The Legacy Cube has been consistently updated over the years during the course of Mike's life. That way, in the event of his death, the most up-to-date version would always be available. The current downloadable version to download is the last complete build that he made. 

Below you can see all previous versions of The Legacy Cube, and what has been added or removed in prior updates.
