What's Included

The Legacy Cube application is separated into three categories; Public, Semi-Private, and Private. From the navigation screen you will be able to access each section of the cube from one of these three areas. Even with a limited guest login, you will have access to a portion of each of these categories.    

1

Public Areas

The submenu found under "Public" are the areas of The Legacy Cube that Mike was known for, vocal about, or is simply a reflection of his public and/or online persona.
2

Semi-Private

This section of The Legacy Cube are the parts of Mike's life that better outline who Mike really was, but not the parts of himself that he shared with everyone. 
3

Private

This portion of The Legacy Cube centers around the more intimate side of Mike. It spans as far as his failures to his success, and encompasses a side of him very little know anything about.
